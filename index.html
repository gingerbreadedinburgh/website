Welcome to Gingerbread Edinburgh and Lothian

We are a dedicated non-profit organization committed to supporting single parent families across Edinburgh and the Lothian region. Our mission is to provide practical support, advice, and resources to help single parents thrive and build stronger, more resilient families.

Our Mission

To champion single parents and their children, providing the support and resources they need to overcome challenges and achieve their goals. We believe every single parent family deserves respect, recognition, and the opportunity to flourish.

Our Values

Empowerment

We believe in empowering single parents to make informed decisions and take control of their lives.

Respect

We treat every family with dignity and respect, regardless of their circumstances.

Community

We foster a sense of community and belonging among single parent families.

Our Services

We offer a comprehensive range of services designed to support single parents at every stage of their journey.

Benefits Advice

Get expert help understanding and claiming the benefits you're entitled to. We'll guide you through the application process and help maximize your income.

Service Charge: Free of charge

One-to-One Support

Personal support sessions with our trained advisors to discuss your specific needs and develop action plans.

Service Charge: Free of charge

Group Support Sessions

Join other single parents in our regular support groups, workshops, and social activities.

Service Charge: Free of charge

Legal Advice

Initial legal advice on family law matters, including child maintenance, custody, and housing issues.

Service Charge: Free initial consultation

Training and Education

Access to training courses, educational opportunities, and skill-building workshops.

Service Charge: Varies by course

Emergency Support

Crisis support including food parcels, emergency funding, and urgent accommodation assistance.

Service Charge: Free of charge
