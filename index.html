Welcome to Gingerbread Edinburgh and Lothian

We are a dedicated non-profit organization committed to supporting single parent families across Edinburgh and the Lothian region. For over 50 years, we have provided practical childcare support, after-school clubs, and holiday programs to help single parents thrive and build stronger, more resilient families.

Quality Childcare & After School Support

Professional after-school clubs and holiday programs across four Edinburgh locations

🏫 After School Clubs

Monday to Friday after-school care at four Edinburgh locations: Dalry, Lorne, St Cuthbert's, and Tollcross primary schools.

🎯 Holiday Programs

Full-day holiday clubs during school breaks with activities, trips, and professional supervision from 8:30am to 5:25pm.

👨‍👩‍👧‍👦 Family Support

Dedicated support for single parent families with flexible booking options and childcare voucher acceptance.

Our Services

We provide comprehensive childcare services across Edinburgh to support working single parents.

After School Clubs

What's Included:

  • Professional supervision and activities
  • Healthy snacks (fruit provided)
  • Homework support
  • Educational games and activities
  • Safe, nurturing environment

What to Bring: Packed lunch and nut-free snack

Holiday Clubs

Full Day Care: 8:30am - 5:25pm (Collection by 5:30pm)

Activities Include:

  • Educational trips and outings
  • Arts and crafts
  • Sports and games
  • Structured learning activities

Advance Booking Required

In-Service Day Cover

We provide cover for Tollcross Primary School in-service days from 8:30am - 5:30pm.

Contact our office for dates and booking.

Additional Support

We Accept:

  • Childcare vouchers through employers
  • Universal Credit support
  • Job Centre payments
  • College/University funding streams

Important Notes

Public Holidays: We are closed on public holidays - please check with office or play leader for specific dates.

Trip Costs: Parents may be asked to contribute to trip costs, payable in advance via internet banking.
