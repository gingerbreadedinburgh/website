About Us

Supporting Single Parent Families Since 1985

For over four decades, Gingerbread Edinburgh & Lothian has been a trusted partner for single parent families across Edinburgh. As a registered Scottish charity, we understand the unique challenges facing single parents.

We provide professional, affordable childcare solutions that enable parents to work, study, and build better futures for their families — from four convenient school-based locations across Edinburgh.

School-based after-school clubs at 4 Edinburgh primary schools
Reduced fees specifically designed for single parent families
Full-day holiday and in-service day cover
Accepts childcare vouchers, Universal Credit & Job Centre payments
