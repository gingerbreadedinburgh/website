Your Impact

What Your Donation Supports

Your generosity directly funds the services that make a real difference to Edinburgh's single parent families.

🍎

Healthy snacks and nutritious fruit for children every day at our clubs

🎨

Better equipment, art supplies, and learning resources for our clubs

🎮

Educational games, books, and structured learning activities

🚌

Educational trips and outings during our holiday club programmes

👩‍🏫

Training and development for our staff to deliver the best possible care

💚

Keeping fees affordable so no single parent family is priced out of care
