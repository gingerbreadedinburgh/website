Our Services

Comprehensive childcare solutions across Edinburgh, built around the lives of working single parents.

🏃

After School Clubs

Monday to Friday · 4 Edinburgh Primary Schools

What's Included

  • Professional supervision and activities
  • Healthy snacks (fruit provided daily)
  • Educational games and activities
  • Arts, crafts and creative play
  • Safe, nurturing environment
  • Qualified and caring staff throughout

📋 What to Bring

Packed lunch and a nut-free snack for your child each day.

📍 Available At

Dalry Primary School

Lorne Primary School

St Cuthbert's Primary School

Tollcross Primary School

🌞

Holiday Clubs

Full-Day Care · 8:30am – 5:25pm · Advance Booking Required

Activities Include

  • Educational trips and outings
  • Arts and crafts sessions
  • Sports and outdoor games
  • Structured learning activities
  • Creative projects and workshops
  • Themed activity days

🕐 Hours

Drop-off from: 8:30am

Collection by: 5:30pm (latest)

Venue: Tollcross Primary School

⚠️ Important

Advance booking is required. A £40 per day penalty applies if a child attends without a confirmed booking.

Parents may be asked to contribute to trip costs, payable in advance via internet banking.

📅

In-Service Day Cover

Tollcross Primary School · 8:30am – 5:30pm

What to Expect

  • Full-day supervised care
  • Structured activities throughout the day
  • Same high standard as holiday clubs
  • Professional and caring staff
  • Healthy snacks provided

📍 Venue

Tollcross Primary School only

117–119 Fountainbridge, Edinburgh EH3 9QG

Important Notes

🏖️ Public Holidays

We are closed on all public holidays. Please check with the office or your play leader for specific closure dates.

🎒 Trip Costs

Parents may be asked to contribute to trip costs for holiday club outings. All contributions are payable in advance via internet banking.

⏰ Collection Times

Please ensure your child is collected on time. A late collection fee of £25 applies for persistent late collections.
