📋 Payment Policies

📆 Cancellation Policy

Full week's notice is required to cancel your child's place. Failure to give notice means fees are still due.

🚫 No Shows

You must pay for all booked days even if your child does not attend. No exceptions apply.

💰 Credits

Unused credits are kept on your account for future use. No cash reimbursements are made.

⏱️ Late Collection

A £25 fee applies for continuous late collections. Please make arrangements to collect on time.

⚠️ Holiday Club Penalty

£40 per day applies if a child attends the holiday club without a confirmed prior booking.

📅 Admin Fee

Annual admin fee of £25 per family is due by 31st March each year. One fee per family only.

Have Questions About Fees?

Our team is happy to help you understand the fee structure and explore payment options available to your family.
