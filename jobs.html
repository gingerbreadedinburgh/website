Work With Us

Join our dedicated team and help make a difference in the lives of single parent families across Edinburgh and Lothian.

Current Opportunities

Paid Roles

We are always looking for passionate individuals to join our team. Experience working with children and a love of making a difference are key.

Paid · Part-Time

Play Leaders

Lead activities and provide professional supervision at our after-school and holiday clubs. You'll plan engaging sessions and create a safe, nurturing environment for children aged 5–11.

Paid · Part-Time

After School Club Assistants

Support play leaders in delivering high-quality after-school care across our four Edinburgh locations. Flexible hours that fit around your schedule.

Paid · Seasonal

Holiday Club Coordinators

Coordinate and deliver our holiday club programmes at Tollcross Primary. Plan trips, organise activities, and ensure a fun, safe experience for every child.

Paid · Flexible

Administrative Support

Help keep our operations running smoothly — managing bookings, parent communications, and admin tasks from our Tollcross base.

📋 Requirements

Experience working with children, relevant qualifications (or willingness to obtain them), and a genuine passion for supporting families.

All roles require a Protecting Vulnerable Groups (PVG) check.

Give Back

Volunteer With Us

Perfect for students, retirees, or anyone who wants to give back to the community. Every hour you give makes a real difference.

🎨

Activity Support

Help run arts, crafts and activities during after-school and holiday clubs alongside our paid staff.

🚌

Trip Assistance

Support children and staff during educational outings and holiday club day trips across Edinburgh.

📋

Admin Help

Provide administrative support to help our small team manage bookings, communications and records.

🎉

Special Events

Help organise and run fundraising events, community activities, and special celebration days.
